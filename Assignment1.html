Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean Augusse-Dominique-Ingres


The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Augusse Dominique Ingres and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year


Date: 1806

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimensions 100 x 70 cm

Home: Malbe dx Lowere
